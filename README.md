# UKButtercup

My ULTRAKILL modding template . . . . . Under construction .

This is not only a modding template . . . It is, additionally, a unity package you can import, which will make modding ULTRAKILL much, much easier .

## Planned Features :
- Advanced custom level support .
- Advanced custom enemy support .
- Advanced custom weapons support .
- Advanced plugin config support .
- Advanced custom settings/keybinds support ( in vanilla menu )
- Advanced scene changing support ( replacing items/textures in vanilla scene, adding to vanilla scene, etc. )

If this seems ambitious, just know that I already figured most of this stuff out a while ago . . . Just needs to be implemented graphically and in TUI .

Shouldn't be *too* difficult . . .
